About Us

The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science offers exciting and high quality undergraduate and graduate degree programs and certificates. At the undergraduate level, we offer multiple degrees and emphasis areas in mathematics including data science and fiscal mathematics, and three degrees on the computing side: computer science, computing technology, and cybersecurity.
